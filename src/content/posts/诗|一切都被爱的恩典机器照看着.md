> 高中时非常喜欢的一首小诗。当时被其中所描述的技术乐观主义深深打动了。这可是1967年的作品呀。

<br/>



### 由爱的恩典机器照管一切(1967)

##### [Richard Brautigan](https://allpoetry.com/Richard-Brautigan) 

<br/>

I like to think (and the sooner the better!)

<font color=gray>我常常想象（越快越好！）</font>

of a cybernetic meadow

<font color=gray>在一块电子草地上</font>

where mammals and computers

<font color=gray>哺乳动物和电脑</font>

live together in mutually

<font color=gray>共同生活在一起</font>

programming harmony

<font color=gray>和谐地编程</font>

like pure water

<font color=gray>如水般纯净</font>

touching clear sky.

<font color=gray>触碰清澈的天空</font>

<br/>

I like to think(right now, please!)

<font color=gray>我常常幻想（请就在此刻实现！）</font>

of a cybernetic forest

<font color=gray>在一片电子森林中</font>

filled with pines and electronics

<font color=gray>布满了松树和电子</font>

where deer stroll peacefully past computers

<font color=gray>小鹿平静地在电脑边散步</font>

as if they were flowers with spinning blossoms.

<font color=gray>在盛开的花丛中一般</font>

<br/>

I like to think(it has to be!)

<font color=gray>我常常期盼（它必须实现！）</font>

of a cybernetic ecology

<font color=gray>一个电子生态圈</font>

where we are free of our labors

<font color=gray>我们从自然劳作中解放出来</font>

and joined back to nature,

<font color=gray>回归自然</font>

returned to our mammal

<font color=gray>回到我们的哺乳动物</font>

brothers and sisters,

<font color=gray>兄弟姐妹们身边</font>

and all watched over by machines of loving grace.

<font color=gray>一切都被那慈爱的机器照看着</font>
